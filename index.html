Ather

Ather Energy Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian electric vehicle company founded by Tarun Mehta and Swapnil Jain in 2013. It manufactures two electric scooters - Ather 450X and Ather 450 Plus. It has made electric vehicle charging infrastructure called Ather Grid

Revolt Motors

Revolt Motors is an Indian Two-Wheeler company producing Electric motor bikes in India.This Company was founded by Rahul Sharma, an Indian Businessman.They created India's First AI Enabled Motorcycle in two different models RV-300 and RV-400.

Website

Tork Motors

Tork Motors in one of its kind EV Startup which mainly targets the racing industry with their keen interest in the bikes segment competing with the fast growing market.The Tork T6X is India's first electric performance motorcycle.

Website

Evolet

Gurugram based start-up Evolet India is a product of veteran defence officers, Col.Ajay Ahlawat and Kamaljit Kataria, who wanted to find a solution to avoid reaching out to fuel stations from their far flung secluded cantonments.

Website

Euler

Saurav Kumar quit a high paying job abroad and came to India to set up the EV Startup Euler Motors which currently focusses on affordable electric three wheelers for the Indian market and accelerating the transition to new age electric vehicles
