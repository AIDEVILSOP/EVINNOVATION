Major EV Start-ups

India is a country of brilliant minds and determined bodies, here we showcase some of the major EV start-ups which are the result of the relentless hardwork of Indians amongst us.


Ather

Ather 450x

Ather 450plus

Ather Energy Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian electric vehicle company founded by Tarun Mehta and Swapnil Jain in 2013. It has manufactured two electric scooters - the Ather 450X and the Ather 450 Plus. Ather Energy is currently valued at $187M (Rs. 1,408 crore). Ather Energy also establishes Ather Grid, an electric vehicle charging infrastructure in cities it is present in. The company has set up over 220 fast charging points in 23 cities across India as of November 2021.

Revolt Motors

Revolt RV300

Revolt RV400

Revolt Motors is the next-gen mobility company, created for the smart world. With technology at its roots and a class-apart product engineering, Revolt has introduced India’s first AI-enabled motorcycle without compromising on the performance or aesthetics of a regular ride. The Revolt Motors valuation stands at ₹1.5B in funding over 1 round.

Tork Motors

Tork T6x

Tork TX 04

Tork Motors in one of its kind EV Startup which mainly targets the racing industry with their keen interest in the bikes segment competing with the fast growing market. It is a high-performance electric vehicle and charging infrastructure manufacturing startup based out of Pune, India. Backed by a successful racing background and 8 years of groundbreaking research. Tork motors valuation stands at $9.13M. One of it’s investors is Rattan Tata.

Evolet

Evolet Pony

Evolet Derby

Gurugram based electric vehicle start up Evolet India s a product of veteran defence officers, Col. Ajay Ahlawat, Sqn Ldr Prerana Chaturvedi and Kamaljeet Kataria, a full time businessman who wanted to build a mobility solution to avoid reaching out to fuel stations from their far flung, secluded cantonments. Evolet Valuation & Funding stands at $300K. One of it’s investors is Innotech Ventures.

Euler Motors

Euler HiLoad

Purnia born Saurav Kumar quit a high paying job abroad and came back to India to set up the EV Startup Euler Motors which currently focusses on affordable electric three wheelers for the Indian market. Euler Motors has raised a pre-Series A, funding of $2.2M, with the angel investment form Andrew Lee.
